Dependable

The sites definition for this strength is “Trust is important to you, and you care about being seen as responsible and trustworthy. People count on you to do what you say you will do. When you make a promise, you mean to keep it.”. I think that this strength shows that whoever has it sticks to their word. Somebody in my life that could represent this skill is Amir Khan, he always does what he says he is going to do. I can organize my time better and keep more promises in order to improve this strength. I sometimes go out of my way to make sure that I do what I say I will do. I would say that I stick to my word.
