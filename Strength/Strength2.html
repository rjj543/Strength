Confidence

The sites definition for this strength is “You believe in yourself and what you can do. This helps you take on challenges because you feel sure you can succeed.”. I think that this strength shows that whoever has it takes on challenges commonly. Somebody in my life that could represent this skill is Ben Schwartz, he often does different things, solely to see peoples reactions. I can speak for what I believe in right and take a speaking role in a program in order to improve this strength. I will often take on stupid challenges just to try them and dont stop until the result is good. I would say that I am determined and rarely give up.
