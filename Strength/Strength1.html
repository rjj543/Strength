Discoverer

The sites definition for this strength is “A thinker and learner, you are excited about exploring ideas and making connections. You like to ask the questions "How?" and "Why?"”. I think that this strength shows that whoever has it is eager to learn more things and what they can impact. Somebody in my life that could represent this skill is Timothy Golubowski, he commonly asks questions in chemistry and asks everyone why they are being stupid. I can figure out how things work and research them further in order to improve this strength. I will occasionally ask in class why certain things happen, which kind of displays this. I would say that I like to see how certain things function and break them down as a strength.
